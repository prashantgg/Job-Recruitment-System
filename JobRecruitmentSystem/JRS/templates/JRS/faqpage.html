Frequently Asked Questions

Is my data secure?

Ans: Yes, we use advanced encryption and security protocols to protect your data.

What roles are available in the system?

Ans: Candidates and recruiters.

Can I reset my password if I forget it?

Ans: Yes, use the "Forgot Password" option on the login page.

Is there a fee to use the system?

Ans: No, it’s free for both candidates and recruiters.

How do I contact support?

Ans: Use the "Contact Us" form or email us at future_fits@gmail.com.

How do I post a job?

Ans: Log in as a recruiter and use the "Post Job" option on your dashboard.

Can I edit a job posting?

Ans: Yes, go to the "Manage Jobs" section, select the job, and click "Edit."

How do I view candidate applications?

Ans: Under each job posting, you’ll find a list of applicants with their details.

How do I remove a job post?

Ans: Navigate to "Manage Jobs," select the job, and click "Delete."

How do I register as a candidate?

Ans: Go to the "Register" page, fill in your details, select "Candidate" as your role, and submit the form.

Can I track my job applications?

Ans: Yes, your dashboard displays all applications and their statuses (e.g., applied, shortlisted, rejected).

How do I upload my CV?

Ans: Log in, go to your profile, and use the "Upload CV" option.
